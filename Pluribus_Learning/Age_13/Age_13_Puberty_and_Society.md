Google search summary 2025-12-14: "What's Happening to Me? The Answers to Some of the World's Most Embarrassing Questions is a classic, humorous, and empathetic children's book about puberty, written by Peter Mayle (author of Where Did I Come From?) and illustrated by Arthur Robins, with design by Paul Walter, first published in 1975. It helps pre-teens understand physical and emotional changes like acne, wet dreams, periods, and voice changes in a relatable way, making it a helpful guide for kids and parents to start conversations about adolescence."
